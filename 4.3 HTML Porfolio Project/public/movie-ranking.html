The Best Movies According to Moi

My top 3 movies/series of all time

Fantasy: Harry Potter series

Rating: 5/5
Out of all the fantasy stories and movies, the wizarding world is the most imaginative and the one I want to live in the most.

Horror: The Pope's Exorcist

Rating: 5/5
This horror movie follows logical action and it is true heroic evil fighting. Avoids cheap scares and digusting material.

Sci-Fi: Ready Player One

Rating: 4/5
All out 80's extravaganza, but the book is honestly just better.
